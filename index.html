Services for Libraries

Helping you to go online

We can help you at the new stage of your library’s development and arrange a library service online. Thanks to that, you will be able to render services to library readers remotely, as well as contact online your colleagues from other libraries throughout the world. With the round-the-clock access and user-friendly search engine, users will quickly find anything they need – from anywhere and at any time. You can also get feedback and systemize it to see what fields of your work require enhancement

Digitalizing books

We can convert your books to the digital – fast and in the required resolution. We use high-tech scanners capable of 1,000+ pages per hour. To speed up your going online even further, you can use our extensive digital book base. We scan rare and illustrated books with high resolution, saving all the printing details

Offering library-card-based advice

We can compile a base of your offline and online readers. Using their reading history, we can offer online service users and librarians reading selection advice. This can help find the appropriate literature in mere minutes

Establishing a dedicated community of fellow thinkers

Harry Potter fans and admirers of Terry Pratchett, contemporary poetry lovers and adherers of historic memoirs – all of them are there, attending your library. We know how to find and bring them together, as well as to setup an event schedule for their community for them to share their thoughts about books they have read, give personal advice, and discuss novelties. Thus, your library will have its own book club

Services for Book Stores

Offering personal advice to your buyers

We can design a unique book rating system for you and engage clubs of readers and literary professionals. Visitors of online book stores will be offered purchase advice based on assortment renewal and actions of other buyers. We do not just offer world bestsellers but also collector’s pieces and dedicated literature. Apart from displaying novelties and popular goods, you will be able to customize pop-up windows offering books that you need to sell first along with campaign products

Offering a keyword-based book search

With it, buyers will be able to find any book they need even if they do not know its author or full name. We can develop keyword selection recommendations for you based on literary genre names, fields of reference, names of central characters, book publication times, and many other parameters for the search to take mere seconds. This will save your buyers’ time, and they will feel pleased about attending your store

Offering an online platform

We can make a virtual copy of your book store – an Internet platform with book notes, readers’ ratings and opinions, a user-friendly payment engine, and anything else you might need to succeed. Your audience will go beyond your city, and thousands of buyers will be able to purchase your books regardless the distance
